Melanie Dunn is a marketing strategist and systems builder at Three Seven Marketing, where she leads SEO, AI optimization, and content strategy for B2B companies with complex offerings. She has 10+ years of experience helping technical companies in life sciences, manufacturing, software, and research build marketing that gets found and gets results.


Melanie also creates , a content brand for working moms learning marketing, AI, and automation in plain language. She builds AI-powered tools and workflows, including Pip, a custom AI project manager used at Three Seven, and a Claude MCP-based meal planning system documented at melaniedunn.work.


She lives in Ohio. Available for virtual and in-person speaking, podcast appearances, and panel discussions.
